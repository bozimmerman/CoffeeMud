Apprentice

DescriptionAn Apprentice may not go past level 1, and qualifies only for the games most
common non-combat skills. You are free to move around in the game like
anyone else, in order to learn some of the ropes before choosing a more
permanent career. When you have decided which of the main classes to go to,
you may use the TRAIN command with an appropriate teacher to become the class
of your choosing. If you can't find an appropriate teacher, remember that a
player of the proper class may be able to help you. Be sure to save yourself
a training session for this purpose!
Prime StatisticWisdom
QualificationsWisdom 5+, Intelligence 5+
Practices5 +[(Wisdom/6)+5 per lvl]
Training Points6 +1 per level
Hit Points20 +[(Con/9)+(1d4) /lvl]
Mana100 +[(Int/10)+(1d2)/lvl]
Movement100 +[5X(Str/18) /lvl]
Attack Skill+(Wis/18)-1 per level
Damage Bonus+1 damage per 10 level(s)
Other BonusesGains lots of xp for training to a new class, and gets bonus common skills.
WeaponsMust use dagger-like or natural weapons.
ArmorMust wear cloth, vegetation, or paper based armor.
Other LimitationsA player may not gain more than 1 levels in this class.
Starting Equipmenta small dagger
