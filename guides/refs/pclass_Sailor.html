Sailor

DescriptionSailors are the workmen of the high seas. These common folk spend their
lives gathering resources, traveling, transporting goods, and exploring seas,
rivers, and lakes. On large sailing vessels, their skills are unparalleled,
and no ship should be without plenty of them.
Prime StatisticDexterity
QualificationsConstitution 5+, Dexterity 5+
Practices5 +[(Wisdom/6)+1 per lvl]
Training Points3 +1 per level
Hit Points20 +[(Con/6)+(1d5) /lvl]
Mana100 +[(Int/9)+(1d2)/lvl]
Movement100 +[5X(Str/18) /lvl]
Attack Skill+(Dex/18)-1 per level
Damage Bonus+1 damage per 15 level(s)
Other BonusesSailors earn twice as much XP as other commoners when trawling, sea explore xp, and double experience in ship combat.
WeaponsMay use any weapons.
ArmorMust wear cloth, vegetation, or paper based armor.
Other LimitationsNone
Starting Equipmenta belaying pin
